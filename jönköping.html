Bars and places to go

Where should I eat?

In the city there is many restaurants and the best way to find something you like is just to walk along the streets and read the diffrenent menues and food chooices.
Two places I have tried and would recommend is Sambosak and Basilika. Both of them is placed along the street with all the stores in the middle of the city.
Sambosak is an indonesian restaurant. They have vegetarian alternatives so this is a good restaurant whatever food habits you might have.
They have different Mezas that is smaller portions of food like pots, salads and sauces. I would personally say that they have something for everybody.
Basilika is an asian restaurant. This restaurant offers a buffet, but you can also just order specific diches to if you don't want the buffet.
In the buffet they have common asian dishes, suchi and of coaurse they also offers fruit and dessert. If you're into asian food this is a really good choice for you.
