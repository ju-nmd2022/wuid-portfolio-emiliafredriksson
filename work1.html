Stylization Minnie Mouse

When?

Created 2022-10-11

What?

This is an stylization of Minnie Mouse. It's created in Adobe Illustrator for a school assignment.

Why?

The assignment were to make a stylization of something. When making a stylization it's important to think of what you need to keep for people to understand what it is, and what you can delete. I choose to do a stylization of Minnie Mouse because she was in one of my favorite shows when I were a kid. The one thing that I thought were important for people to understand what character I made a stylization of was her bow.
